Passenger Name Entry (Amadeus Format)

Fill in passenger and booking details to generate Amadeus commands.

Command Descriptions:

  • NM: Name element (adults, children, infants)
  • AP: Phone contact (e.g. AP YYC403-6152322)
  • APE: Email address of the passenger
  • TKTL: Ticketing time limit (e.g. TLTL15APR)
  • RF: Received from (agent or customer name)
  • ER: End and Redisplay the PNR
  • IG: Ignore changes
  • IEPJ-EMLA: Itinerary receipt via email format
  • ITL-EML: Email itinerary to provided address
  • DO1: Display operational details of flight segment 1
